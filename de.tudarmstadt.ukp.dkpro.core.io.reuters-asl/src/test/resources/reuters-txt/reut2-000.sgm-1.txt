STANDARD OIL <SRD> TO FORM FINANCIAL UNIT

Standard Oil Co and BP North America Inc said they plan to form a venture to manage the money market borrowing and investment activities of both companies.     BP North America is a subsidiary of British Petroleum Co Plc <BP>, which also owns a 55 pct interest in Standard Oil.     The venture will be called BP/Standard Financial Trading and will be operated by Standard Oil under the oversight of a joint management committee.   Reuter &#3;
